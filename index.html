PhD Student | Computer Science | Virginia Tech

Hi there! My name is Juno and I am a first-year PhD student in the REACH Lab at VT. My research is on Computer Science Education in rural communities. My goal is to make technology and society more equitable toward minoritized communities. I strive to use asset-based and participatory methodologies in my research. I am also interested in working with Autistic, ADHD, and Disabled students. This will allow CS to be more accessible and bring diversity of thought into our field.
