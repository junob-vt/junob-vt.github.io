PhD Student | Computer Science | Virginia Tech

Hi there! My name is Juno and I am a first-year PhD student in the REACH Lab at VT. My current research is on Computer Science Education in rural communities. I hope to understand how rural parents conceptualize computing education and STEM careers for their children. I strive to use asset-based, learner-centered, and participatory methodologies in my research. My goal is to make technology and society more equitable toward minoritized communities. I also hope to work with neurodivergent and disabled students to make CS more accessible as well as increase cognitive diversity in our field.
